This is the first entry in a new section of the site for short, technical notes — the things that don't quite warrant a blog post but are worth writing down anyway.

Expect things like:

- **Build logs** — what I'm shipping in [QuantSense](https://github.com/Metbcy/quantsense), [bomdrift](https://github.com/Metbcy/bomdrift), and [SecureScan](https://github.com/Metbcy/securescan), and the design decisions behind them.
- **Reading log** — papers, blog posts, or RFCs worth signal-boosting, with a sentence or two on why.
- **Debugging stories** — short writeups of bugs that took longer than they should have, so future-me can find them with a search.

No publishing schedule, no comments, no newsletter — just a place to leave bread crumbs.

If you want to follow along, [GitHub](https://github.com/Metbcy) is the best place — most of these notes will reference work happening in the open there.
